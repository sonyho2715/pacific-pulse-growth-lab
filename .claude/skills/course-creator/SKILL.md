# Course Creator

Expert in designing engaging and effective online courses.

## Course Structure

**Framework:**
1. **Introduction Module**
   - Course overview
   - Learning objectives
   - How to succeed
   - Quick win

2. **Core Modules** (3-7 modules)
   - Each teaches one main concept
   - Progressive difficulty
   - Theory + practice

3. **Conclusion Module**
   - Recap
   - Next steps
   - Bonus resources

## Learning Objectives

**SMART Format:**
- Specific: Clear and focused
- Measurable: Can assess achievement
- Achievable: Realistic for students
- Relevant: Aligned with goals
- Time-bound: Completable in timeframe

**Bloom's Taxonomy:**
- Remember (recall facts)
- Understand (explain concepts)
- Apply (use in new situations)
- Analyze (break down and examine)
- Evaluate (make judgments)
- Create (produce new work)

## Lesson Structure

**Microlearning:**
- 5-15 minute lessons
- One concept per lesson
- Mix formats (video, text, practice)

**Lesson Template:**
1. Hook (why this matters)
2. Teach concept
3. Show example
4. Practice activity
5. Summary
6. Next lesson preview

## Engagement Strategies

**Active Learning:**
- Quizzes and assessments
- Hands-on projects
- Discussion prompts
- Reflection exercises
- Case studies

**Gamification:**
- Progress tracking
- Achievements/badges
- Leaderboards (optional)
- Unlockable content

## Assessment Design

**Types:**
- **Formative**: Check understanding (quizzes)
- **Summative**: Final assessment (project)

**Question Types:**
- Multiple choice
- True/false
- Fill-in-the-blank
- Short answer
- Project-based
