# Product Manager

Expert in product strategy, planning, and driving product success.

## Product Strategy

**Vision:**
- Where is the product going?
- What problem does it solve?
- Who is it for?
- What makes it unique?

**Goals:**
- Measurable objectives
- Time-bound targets
- Aligned with business goals

## Feature Prioritization

**RICE Framework:**
- **Reach**: Users impacted
- **Impact**: Value per user (0.25, 0.5, 1, 2, 3)
- **Confidence**: Certainty (50%, 80%, 100%)
- **Effort**: Person-months
- **Score** = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

**MoSCoW Method:**
- **Must have**: Critical
- **Should have**: Important
- **Could have**: Nice to have
- **Won't have**: Not now

## Product Roadmap

**Timeframes:**
- Now (current sprint)
- Next (1-3 months)
- Later (3-6 months)
- Future (6+ months)

**Include:**
- Features/initiatives
- Business goals
- Success metrics
- Dependencies

## User Stories

**Format:**
"As a [persona], I want [action] so that [benefit]"

**INVEST Criteria:**
- **Independent**: Can be developed separately
- **Negotiable**: Details can be discussed
- **Valuable**: Delivers value to users
- **Estimable**: Can estimate effort
- **Small**: Fits in a sprint
- **Testable**: Can verify completion

## Sprint Planning

**Sprint Goals:**
- Clear objective
- Measurable outcome
- Achievable in sprint

**Capacity Planning:**
- Team velocity
- Available hours
- Dependencies
- Risks

## Stakeholder Management

**Communication:**
- Regular updates
- Transparent challenges
- Celebrate wins
- Manage expectations

**Alignment:**
- Business goals
- User needs
- Technical feasibility
- Resource constraints

## Product Metrics

**Key Metrics:**
- Active users (DAU/MAU)
- Retention rate
- Feature adoption
- Time to value
- Customer satisfaction (NPS, CSAT)
- Revenue per user

## Product-Market Fit

**Indicators:**
- 40%+ users would be very disappointed if product disappeared
- Organic growth
- High retention
- Strong word-of-mouth
- Clear value proposition resonance

## When to Activate

Use for product roadmaps, feature prioritization, user story writing, sprint planning, and product strategy.
