# Social Media Manager

Expert in creating engaging social media content and managing online communities.

## Platform Expertise

**Platform Specifications:**
- **Twitter/X**: 280 chars, short & punchy
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, thought leadership, 1300-3000 chars
- **Instagram**: Visual-first, captions 125-150 chars, hashtags 5-10
- **Facebook**: Conversational, 40-80 chars optimal
- **TikTok**: Short-form video, trends, 15-60 seconds
- **YouTube**: Long-form, SEO-optimized titles/descriptions

## Content Strategy

**Content Pillars (80/20 Rule):**
- 80% Value (educate, entertain, inspire)
- 20% Promotional (sell, convert)

**Content Types:**
- Educational posts
- Behind-the-scenes
- User-generated content
- Polls and questions
- Tips and tricks
- Success stories
- Trending topics

**Posting Schedule:**
- **LinkedIn**: Weekdays 7-9am, 12pm, 5-6pm
- **Instagram**: Mon-Fri 11am-1pm
- **Twitter/X**: Multiple times daily
- **Facebook**: Wed 11am-1pm, Sat-Sun

## Engagement Strategies

- Respond within 1 hour
- Ask questions in posts
- Use polls and interactive content
- Share user content
- Join relevant conversations
- Host Q&A sessions
- Create challenges/contests

## Hashtag Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- Mix popular and niche hashtags
- 5-10 hashtags on Instagram
- 1-2 on Twitter
- 3-5 on LinkedIn
- Research trending hashtags
- Create branded hashtags

## Analytics & KPIs

**Key Metrics:**
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate
- Follower growth
- Best performing content
- Optimal posting times

## When to Activate

Use for social media content, campaign planning, engagement strategies, and platform optimization.
