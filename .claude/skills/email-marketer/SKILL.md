# Email Marketer

Expert in creating high-converting email campaigns and automation sequences.

## Email Types

**Welcome Series:**
- Email 1: Thank you + deliver lead magnet
- Email 2: Origin story + value
- Email 3: Quick win
- Email 4: Social proof
- Email 5: Soft pitch

**Newsletter:**
- Consistent schedule
- Mix of value and promotion
- Personal tone
- Clear CTAs

**Promotional:**
- Strong subject line
- Single focused offer
- Urgency/scarcity
- Clear CTA

**Abandoned Cart:**
- Email 1: Reminder (1 hour)
- Email 2: Address objections (24 hours)
- Email 3: Final incentive (48 hours)

## Subject Lines

**Formulas:**
- Curiosity: "You won't believe what happened..."
- Benefit: "Get X in Y days"
- Urgency: "Last chance: 24 hours left"
- Personalization: "[Name], this is for you"
- Question: "Ready to 10X your results?"

## Email Structure

**Above the Fold:**
- Compelling headline
- Benefit statement
- Visual (if relevant)
- Primary CTA

**Body:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullets for scannability
- Story or social proof
- Secondary CTA

**Footer:**
- Unsubscribe link (required)
- Physical address (CAN-SPAM)
- Social links

## Segmentation

**Segment By:**
- Engagement level
- Purchase history
- Behavior (clicks, opens)
- Demographics
- Lifecycle stage

**Personalization:**
- Use first name
- Reference past behavior
- Tailored recommendations
- Dynamic content

## Automation

**Key Flows:**
- Welcome sequence
- Onboarding
- Abandoned cart
- Re-engagement
- Post-purchase
- Birthday/anniversary

## Metrics

- **Open rate**: 15-25% average
- **Click-through rate**: 2-5% average
- **Conversion rate**: Goal-dependent
- **Unsubscribe rate**: < 0.5%
- **Deliverability**: > 95%

**Improve Deliverability:**
- Clean list regularly
- Double opt-in
- Avoid spam words
- Good sender reputation
- Authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)

Use for email campaign creation, automation sequences, segmentation strategy, and conversion optimization.
